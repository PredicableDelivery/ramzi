Notes for execution

What translates well, what needs care

  • Palette and type stay identical. Cranberry #C43050 as the accent (used sparingly, never as the dominant background), petrol blue #0F2A36 / #132F3C as the ground, Source Serif 4 for headlines that need to feel editorial, DM Sans for labels and UI.
  • Brand mark is the anchor. The AZ monogram appears as the profile picture, the centre tile of the grid, and the cover of any "About" highlight. It is the one element that should never change colour or face direction.
  • Quotes get the editorial treatment. Italic Source Serif 4, a faint cranberry quote-mark in the corner, attribution in tiny tracked DM Sans — same recipe as the website's testimonials section. Easy to template.
  • Don't repeat the wall-logo photograph. On the website it's a hero asset; on Instagram it would compete with the AZ monogram and the brand mark would lose force. Reserve photography for behind-the-scenes / event reels.
  • Arabic posts need their own typography. Tajawal for headlines, Noto Naskh Arabic for body, mirrored layout (right-aligned, RTL flex). The pillar tile in this concept hints at it via the العربية pill.
  • Production: these are HTML mockups, not 1080 × 1080 PNGs. For real posts, export each tile as a square PNG/JPG from Figma using the same colour and type tokens, or have the designer recreate them from this spec.
