05 Legal Consultancy

Trusted external counsel, without the overhead

AZ Legal Solutions provides strategic legal advice across a broad range of practice areas, acting as a trusted external counsel for businesses that need reliable legal input without the overhead of an in-house team.

Scope of advisory

The firm advises on corporate and commercial law, banking and finance, media and entertainment, employment matters, regulatory compliance, and private international law. Clients include UK-based companies, international organisations, NGOs, and individuals navigating complex legal situations.

Flexible engagement

The consultancy model is flexible by design, whether a client needs a one-off legal opinion, ongoing advisory support, or assistance managing a specific transaction or dispute. AZ Legal Solutions adapts to the scope and pace of the work required. Advice is delivered in a direct, practical manner, with a focus on helping clients understand their legal position and make informed decisions with confidence.

All initial enquiries are handled with confidentiality and without obligation. The firm advises in English and French.
