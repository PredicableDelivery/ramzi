Background

AZ Legal Solutions is led by Ramzi El Azzi, a qualified international lawyer registered with the Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA No. 827663). The firm's managing consultant holds an LLM in Law with Distinction from City, University of London, The City Law School, where he was awarded The City Law School Prize for Excellence in Civil Dispute Resolution, recognising outstanding academic achievement in litigation and dispute resolution.

Additional qualifications include certification in eDiscovery and Relativity document review, and wide expertise in legal translation. With over fifteen years of practice across the UK, Europe, and the Middle East, spanning private practice, international organisations, NGOs, and entertainment and design companies, the firm brings a depth of legal and commercial experience that is genuinely international in scope.

Cross-border expertise

The practice was built to serve clients who operate between legal systems, particularly those with connections to both the UK and international. Ramzi's understanding of both jurisdictions, both legal traditions, and the cultural context that shapes how legal matters are experienced by international families and businesses in the UK, is central to the advisory the firm provides.

Whether the matter involves inheritance across two legal systems, structuring a business for UK-international markets trade, protecting intellectual property internationally, or navigating the intersection of UK immigration law with a client's personal circumstances in international markets, the firm's advice is grounded in direct experience of both legal landscapes.

Languages

AZ Legal Solutions advises and corresponds in English and French. Ramzi holds C2-level proficiency in all three languages, with extensive experience drafting and reviewing legal documents in each. This trilingual capability is not a supplementary service; it is a structural feature of the practice, ensuring clients can communicate complex legal matters in the language in which they are most precise and comfortable.

Sector experience

Beyond the UK-international markets legal corridor, the firm's experience spans a range of sectors: intellectual property across music, entertainment, luxury design, and technology; corporate structuring for startups, social enterprises, and cross-border ventures; commercial contracts for service businesses and distribution networks; and media and entertainment law across the UK and the Middle East, including live events, publishing, and music rights.

This breadth of sector experience allows AZ Legal Solutions to advise not merely on the legal technicalities but on the commercial and practical implications of every decision.
