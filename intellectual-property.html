01 Intellectual Property

Protecting what you create, build, and own

Protecting your intellectual property is one of the most important investments a business can make. AZ Legal Solutions advises individuals, businesses, and creative professionals on the full spectrum of intellectual property law.

Trademarks

On the trademark side, we assist clients with searches and clearance advice before adopting a new brand or name, applications for registration with the UK Intellectual Property Office and across international trademark systems, monitoring for potential infringement, and responding to oppositions or challenges from third parties. We also advise on the assignment and licensing of trademark rights, helping clients commercialise their brands in a legally sound way.

Copyright

In copyright, we advise on ownership, which is not always straightforward, particularly where work is created by employees, freelancers, or in the context of collaboration, as well as on licensing structures, rights clearance, and what to do when copyright is used without authorisation. This is particularly relevant for clients in the creative industries, including music, film, publishing, photography, software, and design.

Patents

On patents, we provide guidance on the patentability of inventions, the registration process, and the strategic value of patent protection as part of a broader IP portfolio. We work with clients to understand not just whether something can be protected, but whether it should be, and how that protection fits into their commercial strategy.

A core business asset

With experience advising clients across the entertainment, music, luxury design, and technology sectors in the UK and internationally, AZ Legal Solutions understands that intellectual property is not merely a legal formality. It is a core business asset that requires active, informed, and proactive management throughout the lifecycle of a business.
