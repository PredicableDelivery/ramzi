07 Document Review

Senior legal analysis for complex litigation documents

In complex litigation, the volume and management of documents can be as consequential as the legal arguments themselves. AZ Legal Solutions offers professional document review services using industry-standard eDiscovery software.

eDiscovery and Relativity

AZ Legal Solutions offers professional document review services for law firms and corporations involved in contentious proceedings, using industry-standard eDiscovery software including Relativity. Documents are reviewed and classified for relevance, responsiveness, legal privilege, and confidentiality, with careful attention to accuracy, consistency, and defensibility.

Beyond classification

The firm has worked on major court cases across the UK, producing detailed summaries and document analyses that support legal teams in building and presenting their cases. This service brings a senior legal perspective to the review process, going beyond mechanical classification to provide meaningful legal analysis of the material under review.
