06 Legal Translation

Legally precise, linguistically fluent, contextually appropriate

Legal translation is a specialist discipline that requires not only fluency in multiple languages but a deep understanding of the legal systems and terminology of each jurisdiction.

Languages and coverage

AZ Legal Solutions provides accurate legal translation across English, French, and Arabic, covering contracts, court documents, corporate filings, regulatory submissions, legal opinions, and correspondence.

Legal precision

The firm's C2-level proficiency in all three languages, combined with extensive experience drafting and reviewing legal documents in each, ensures that translations are not merely linguistic but legally precise and contextually appropriate. This is particularly valuable for international businesses, cross-border transactions, diplomatic and governmental matters, and individuals dealing with legal proceedings or documentation across different jurisdictions.
