02 Company Setup and Corporate Governance

Building the right legal structure from day one

Starting or restructuring a business involves a range of legal obligations that go well beyond filing a registration form. AZ Legal Solutions guides founders, entrepreneurs, and organisations through every stage of the company formation and structuring process.

Formation and structuring

We assist with the registration of private limited companies, Community Interest Companies (CICs), and other corporate structures under UK law, advising on the most appropriate vehicle for the client's commercial purpose, tax position, and long-term objectives. We draft and negotiate shareholders' agreements, setting out clearly how decisions will be made, how shares may be transferred, what protections minority shareholders have, and what happens in the event of a dispute or exit. We advise on share issuance, share transfers, and the implications of bringing new investors or partners into an existing structure.

Corporate governance

Beyond formation, the firm provides ongoing corporate governance support. This includes advising on directors' duties and liabilities, maintaining statutory records in compliance with Companies House requirements, drafting internal governance policies and compliance frameworks, and assisting with board-level decision-making processes. For businesses operating across borders, we advise on cross-jurisdictional structuring and the legal implications of operating in multiple markets.

Regulatory compliance

We also support clients with regulatory compliance, helping businesses understand the rules that apply to their sector, implement the procedures needed to stay compliant, and respond effectively when compliance issues arise. Whether you are setting up a social enterprise, scaling a startup, or reorganising an established business, AZ Legal Solutions ensures your legal foundations are solid and your structure supports your ambitions.
